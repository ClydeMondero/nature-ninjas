What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to long-term shifts and alterations in the Earth's climate patterns, primarily driven by natural processes and human activities. These changes encompass variations in temperature, precipitation, wind patterns, and other climate characteristics over decades or longer.

Organization that promotes climate change awareness and how to lessen it

PAGASA

Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration

helps the Philippines combat climate change by monitoring weather patterns, providing early warnings for typhoons and floods, and conducting climate research.

UNCC

United Nations Climate Change

supports global efforts to address climate change by guiding nations in reducing emissions and adapting to its impacts. It oversees international agreements like the Paris Agreement to encourage collective action.

IPCC

The Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change

The Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) is the United Nations body for assessing the science related to climate change.

NASA

National Aeronautics and Space Administration

NASA plays a crucial role in understanding and addressing climate change by leveraging its advanced technology and expertise in Earth observation.
